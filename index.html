The rules are simple. Either of you or the machine will get to bat first.
While "batting" or "bowling", you can pick a number between 0 and 6.
If the bowling side picks the same number as the batting side, the batsman is considered "out".
Else, the batting team's score increases by whatever number they picked.
0 works differently; the batting team's score increases by whatever value the bowling side picked, except 0 in which case the batting team is out.
Whoever scores the most wins!
